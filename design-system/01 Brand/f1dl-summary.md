## One-Line Definition

Carbon structure, titanium precision, green authority, cyan intelligence, human journey, measurable proof.

## Build Rule

Every asset, component, and page must improve one of:

- Sponsor confidence.
- Sponsor acquisition.
- Sponsor retention.
- Media coverage.
- Fan engagement.
- Search visibility.
- AI discoverability.
- Brand authority.
- Commercial value.

## Design References

Use Apple, Porsche, Nike, SpaceX, Formula One teams, and enterprise SaaS as quality benchmarks only. Do not copy protected visual systems, logos, typography, or official motorsport marks.
