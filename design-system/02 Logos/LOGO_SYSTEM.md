# Logo System

## Current SVG Starters

- `svg/logo-ae-mark.svg`
- `svg/logo-horizontal-dark.svg`
- `svg/logo-horizontal-light.svg`
- `svg/logo-helmet-mark.svg`
- `svg/logo-mobile-compact.svg`

## Required Final Exports

Desktop:

- Horizontal dark SVG.
- Horizontal light SVG.
- Horizontal transparent PNG.
- Wordmark only.
- AE mark only.
- Monochrome black.
- Monochrome white.

Mobile:

- Compact AE mark.
- App icon.
- Rounded square icon.
- Helmet mark.
- Race suit mark.
- Favicon.

Formats:

- SVG.
- PNG at 1x, 2x, 3x.
- PDF.
- EPS.
- AI or Figma source.

## Usage Rules

- Use horizontal logo on desktop headers and press assets.
- Use compact mark on mobile nav, favicon, app icon, helmet, and suit.
- Use one-colour mark for physical applications.
- Keep clear space equal to the height of the AE letterform.
- Do not place the logo directly on busy images without a protective overlay.
- Do not use official Formula One or team visual language.

## Quality Tests

- Legible at 16 px.
- Distinct at 44 px mobile nav size.
- Strong at 120 px app icon size.
- Works in one colour.
- Works on carbon black and white.
- Does not resemble an existing team crest or car manufacturer badge.
