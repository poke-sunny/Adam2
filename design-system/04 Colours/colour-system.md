# Colour System

## Primary

- Carbon Black: `#090909`
- Surface Black: `#111111`
- Elevated Black: `#1C1C1C`
- Border Graphite: `#2B2B2B`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

## Accents

- British Racing Green: `#006A52`
- Electric Cyan: `#00E5FF`
- Titanium: `#AEB4BC`

## Signals

- Success: `#00D26A`
- Warning: `#FFB400`
- Danger: `#FF4040`

## Use Rules

- Carbon is the foundation.
- Titanium is the premium neutral.
- Green communicates authority, progress, sponsor value, and heritage.
- Cyan communicates live data, telemetry, AI, and intelligence.
- Warning and danger are reserved for operational states.
- Do not create a one-note cyan or green interface.

## Contrast Rules

- Body text must pass WCAG AA.
- Use white for primary text on dark surfaces.
- Use titanium for secondary text.
- Do not put cyan body text on black for long passages.
