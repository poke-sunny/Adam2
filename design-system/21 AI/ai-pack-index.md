# AI Pack Index

## Purpose

AI should turn racing, media, sponsor, and analytics inputs into useful decisions and high-quality outputs.

## Required AI Products

- AI Race Assistant.
- AI Sponsor Assistant.
- AI Media Assistant.
- AI Content Generator.
- AI Press Writer.
- AI Fan Concierge.
- AI Coaching Journal.
- AI Knowledge Base.
- AI Search.
- AI Insights.

## Required AI Components

- Chat panel.
- Prompt preset menu.
- Insight card.
- Recommendation engine.
- Draft editor.
- Citation list.
- Confidence indicator.
- Human approval state.
- Action button group.
- Source-data drawer.

## Rules

- AI must cite source data where possible.
- Sponsor-facing AI must require human review.
- AI should suggest next actions.
- Do not invent race results, sponsors, media coverage, or revenue.
- AI should feel like a professional assistant, not a novelty chatbot.
