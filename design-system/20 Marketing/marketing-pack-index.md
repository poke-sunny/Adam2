# Marketing Pack Index

## Purpose

Marketing pages and campaigns turn Adam's journey into sponsor, fan, press, and community momentum.

## Required Marketing Assets

- Homepage hero campaign.
- Sponsor campaign.
- Race weekend campaign.
- Press launch kit.
- STEM outreach campaign.
- Community campaign.
- Merchandise campaign.
- Newsletter templates.
- Partner announcement pack.
- Investor deck assets.

## Required Page Types

- Landing page.
- Campaign page.
- Sponsor conversion page.
- Press announcement page.
- Download page.
- Thank-you page.
- Event page.

## Copy Rules

- Lead with proof.
- Be specific.
- Avoid hype.
- Avoid begging for sponsorship.
- Create urgency through professionalism and momentum.

## Conversion Rules

- One primary action per page.
- Sponsor CTAs must be clear and confident.
- Press downloads must be fast.
- Mobile conversion must be designed first.
