# Mission Control Pack Index

## Purpose

Mission Control is the operating dashboard for Adam Ellahi's racing, media, sponsor, and commercial ecosystem.

## Required Views

- Overview.
- Race Performance.
- Audience Growth.
- Sponsor ROI.
- Social Listening.
- Media Coverage.
- Search Visibility.
- AI Citations.
- Website Analytics.
- Heatmaps.
- Conversions.
- Email Growth.
- Global Audience Map.
- Video Analytics.
- Content Performance.
- Partner Performance.
- Commercial Pipeline.
- Revenue Dashboard.

## Required Components

- Next race countdown.
- Latest result card.
- Sponsor ROI card.
- Audience growth card.
- Media mention feed.
- Search visibility trend.
- AI citation tracker.
- Content performance table.
- Commercial pipeline board.
- AI recommendation stack.

## Mobile Priority

Mobile Mission Control should show:

- Today.
- Alerts.
- Next race.
- Sponsor follow-ups.
- Top metrics.
- AI recommendations.

## Acceptance Gate

Mission Control is ready only when it helps the team decide what to do next.
