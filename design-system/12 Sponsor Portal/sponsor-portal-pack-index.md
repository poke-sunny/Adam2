# Sponsor Portal Pack Index

## Purpose

The Sponsor Portal gives current and prospective partners a private, measurable, executive-grade view of partnership value.

## Required Views

- Executive Overview.
- Exposure Dashboard.
- Audience Insights.
- Content Performance.
- Race Weekend Reports.
- Hospitality.
- Activation Ideas.
- Downloads.
- Agreements.
- Contact.
- AI Sponsor Assistant.

## Required Sponsor Assets

- Sponsor badge.
- ROI report.
- Activation card.
- Exposure chart.
- Audience demographic module.
- Brand lift module.
- Hospitality invitation.
- Downloadable executive summary.
- Media download area.

## Required Actions

- Download report.
- Book activation review.
- Request assets.
- View race weekend report.
- Ask AI Sponsor Assistant.
- Share report internally.

## Rules

- No hard-sell language.
- No unverifiable claims.
- Estimated values must be labelled.
- Sponsor pages must feel calm, credible, and commercially mature.
