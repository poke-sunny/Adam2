# Volume 4 - Mission Control

## Product Definition

Mission Control is the command dashboard for Adam Ellahi's motorsport ecosystem.

It should feel like the internal operating layer of a modern racing programme: performance, media, sponsors, content, commercial pipeline, and intelligence in one place.

## Primary Users

- Adam and family/team.
- Sponsor relations lead.
- Media lead.
- Coach.
- Content producer.
- Commercial partner.

## Metric Rules

Every metric must include:

- Metric name.
- Current value.
- Change over time.
- Date range.
- Source.
- Context sentence.
- Suggested action when useful.

Example:

`Sponsor exposure increased 18 percent over the last 30 days, driven by race recap video reach and LinkedIn reposts. Send the July partner update by Friday.`

## Visual Rules

- Dark enterprise UI.
- No decorative chart junk.
- Use Electric Cyan only for live data, AI, and telemetry.
- Use British Racing Green for positive commercial momentum.
- Use warning and danger colours sparingly.
- Keep chart backgrounds calm.

## Mobile Mission Control

Mobile should not try to replicate the full desktop cockpit.

Mobile priority:

- Today.
- Alerts.
- Next race.
- Sponsor follow-ups.
- Top metrics.
- AI recommendations.

Use drill-down cards and saved views.
