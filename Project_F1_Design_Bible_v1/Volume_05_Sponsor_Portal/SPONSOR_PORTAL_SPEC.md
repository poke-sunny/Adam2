# Volume 5 - Sponsor Portal

## Product Definition

The Sponsor Portal is a private executive experience for current and prospective partners.

It should make sponsors feel informed, valued, and early to an opportunity with measurable upside.

## Sponsor Portal Promise

Your partnership is visible, measurable, activated, and growing.

## Portal Sections

- Executive Overview.
- Partnership Assets.
- Exposure Dashboard.
- Audience Insights.
- Content Performance.
- Race Weekend Reports.
- Hospitality.
- Activation Ideas.
- Downloads.
- Invoices and Agreements.
- Contact and Next Steps.
- AI Sponsor Assistant.

## Executive Overview

Above the fold:

- Partner name.
- Partnership status.
- Current reporting period.
- Total reach.
- Exposure value.
- Key content wins.
- Upcoming activation opportunity.

Required CTA:

- Download Report.
- Book Activation Review.

## Sponsor ROI Reporting

Each report should include:

- Executive summary.
- Campaign period.
- Logo exposure.
- Social reach.
- Engagement.
- Video watch time.
- Website traffic from sponsor content.
- Audience demographics.
- Media mentions.
- Hospitality engagement.
- Next recommendations.

Avoid fake precision. When values are estimated, label them clearly.

## Prospective Sponsor Flow

Stages:

1. Why Adam.
2. Audience and story.
3. Partnership opportunities.
4. Activation examples.
5. Reporting preview.
6. Investment levels.
7. Call booking.
8. Follow-up deck.

The page should create urgency through proof and professionalism, not pressure language.

## Sponsor Assets

Required assets:

- Partner badge.
- Activation card.
- Sponsor lockup area.
- Exposure chart.
- Audience demographic module.
- Race weekend report.
- Social proof carousel.
- Hospitality invite.
- Downloadable one-page PDF.
- Sponsor deck.

## AI Sponsor Assistant

Capabilities:

- Explain sponsor ROI.
- Draft partner update emails.
- Suggest activation ideas.
- Summarize race weekend value.
- Recommend next commercial action.
- Prepare meeting notes.

Guardrails:

- Do not invent results.
- Do not publish externally without review.
- Cite source data inside the portal.
