# Volume 7 - Photography Direction

## Photography Role

Photography carries the emotional proof of the brand. UI creates trust; photography creates belief.

The platform should not only show racing. It should show discipline, learning, sacrifice, engineering, recovery, and progress.

## Art Direction

Composition:

- Use generous negative space for UI overlays.
- Frame Adam as focused and composed.
- Use low angles sparingly for hero authority.
- Use close macro detail for texture and craft.
- Use track geometry, apex lines, and pitlane perspective.

Lighting:

- Natural light where possible.
- Cool technical shadows.
- Controlled highlights on helmet, visor, suit, and carbon.
- Avoid overly warm lifestyle treatment.

Colour:

- Grade toward carbon, titanium, deep green, and restrained cyan highlights.
- Skin tones must remain natural.
- Do not crush shadows so far that detail disappears.

## Image Acceptance Criteria

Accept an image only when:

- It feels specific to motorsport.
- It leaves room for UI when used as a hero.
- It does not look like generic stock.
- It has believable lighting.
- It avoids unlicensed logos and trademarks.
- It supports a sponsor, fan, media, or team story.
